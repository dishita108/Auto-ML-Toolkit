Meet the creators

SAGNIK MUKHERJEE

B.E (Electrical Engineering) | M.Tech (Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering) | Data Scientist | Machine Learning Backend developer for the project



Hi, This is Sagnik; I have completed my Masters in Technology (M.Tech) from Sardar Patel Institute of Technology in the domain of Electronics and Telecommunication and have done several certification courses and projects in the domain of ML and Data Science! The idea of Auto-ML at a very basic stage was to identify the various artifacts generated after the model's automated/self data analysis and identify some ML models that are best suited to predict the outcome as desired in the given task! It is also an interesting educational tool for absolute beginners who are willing to learn more about an end-to-end Data Science project and the specifications of the same. While the project is at its roots, we look to bring about innovative technology and incorporate some amazing ideas so that this can turn out to be a useful product in the market! I would like to thank my teammates - come interns for the project, Dishita Ashar and Sejal Bhudani for contributing their knowledge and building the entire front end and other layouts of this web-app! Cheers to learning and we look forward to more upgrades, so please do stay tuned!
